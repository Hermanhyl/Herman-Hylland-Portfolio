About Me

I am currently developing a portfolio website as I pursue my passion for front-end development. At the end of my first year in a two-year program, I have been immersed in learning web design, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This journey has allowed me to build a solid foundation and gain confidence in these core areas.

On a personal note, I am a calm individual who enjoys a wide range of activities, from gaming to spending time outdoors fishing. I also have a strong passion for digital painting, which complements my interest in front-end development by allowing me to explore creativity in different mediums. While I consider myself social, I am not the most outgoing person. However, I am passionate about what I do and have a genuine interest in front-end development.

My goal is to leverage this knowledge to secure a full-time position as a front-end developer. Additionally, I am excited to start side projects that will further enhance my skills and showcase my creativity. Through these experiences, I aim to contribute to innovative web solutions and grow continuously in this dynamic field.
